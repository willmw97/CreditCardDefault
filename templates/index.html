About the Project

Our project will determine whether or not customers of banks should be approved for credit limit increases or loans using a data set from the University of California Irvine archives. The data set from the University of California Irvine contains a list of credit borrowers in Taiwan from 2005. This data shows the credit histories of customers of a major bank in Taiwan, and if they defaulted the next month.
